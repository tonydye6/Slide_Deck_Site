Web3 Adoption Gap

Web3 Games Failed to Match Web2 Mobile Game Success

Web3 games have failed miserably to achieve the same level of mass user adoption and financial success as traditional Web2 mobile games

High Startup Costs

  • Web3 games cost too much for players versus F2P (free to play)
  • Users need to purchase NFTs or tokens just to begin playing

Speculation Focus

  • Web3 games became speculation vehicles rather than entertainment
  • The "play-to-earn" model prioritized financial returns over enjoyable gameplay, attracting investors rather than gamers

Unsustainable Economics

  • Web3 games relied on new player inflows to sustain their economies
  • When growth slowed, token values collapsed, causing mass player exodus

User Experience Problems

  • Setting up crypto wallets, purchasing tokens, and understanding blockchain created friction for users accustomed to one-click app downloads
  • Web3 games prioritized tokenomics over polished, engaging gameplay

Technical Issues

  • Blockchain limitations created problems with transaction speeds, gas fees, and overall performance that traditional games don't face

Market & Design Challenges

  • Misaligned incentives meant Web3 games struggle with balancing player economics, developer sustainability, and investor returns

Limited Target Audience

  • The overlap between cryptocurrency enthusiasts and gamers is smaller than anticipated, limiting the potential user base

Regulatory Uncertainty

  • Concerns about gambling laws, securities regulations, and taxation have complicated the Web3 gaming landscape

$SPARQ Token Utility

Appendix

$SPARQ TOKEN: NOT A GAMING TOKEN?

The $SPARQ token is a Web3 sports marketplace token that does not directly integrate into our games. This avoids in-game economy inflation/deflation risks while still providing Web3 benefits. Players earn NFTs and digital collectibles in-game that can be traded in the $SPARQ ecosystem.

TOKEN UTILITY WITHOUT IN-GAME INTEGRATION

We've designed a model where $SPARQ tokens enable platform governance, service discounts, staking rewards, and marketplace access without disrupting game balance. This strategy provides sustainable token utility while avoiding out-of-control inflation or deflation in-game, while leveraging the broader $SPARQ ecosystem.

SUSTAINABLE GAME ECONOMICS

By separating our in-game economics from the token economics, we create a more sustainable long-term platform. Game rewards (NFTs, collectibles) can be valued in $SPARQ in secondary markets, creating natural token demand without forcing users to engage with crypto directly. Eventually the rewards never touch the underlying liquidity pool creating a major deflationary mechanic.

REVENUE SHARE MODEL

$SPARQ token holders can stake tokens to earn a share of platform revenue, creating lasting value without relying on speculative token price appreciation. This aligns stakeholder incentives for platform growth rather than short-term token pumps.

THE $SPARQ TOKEN IS A NATIVE YIELD AND GOVERNANCE TOKEN THAT POWERS THE LARGER SPARQ SPORTS COLLECTIBLES ECOSYSTEM

Bitcoin Price After Halving

Appendix

What is Halving?

  • Reward for mining new blocks is halved, reducing the rate at which new bitcoins are generated.
  • Occurs every 4 years (210,000 blocks). Last halving: April 2024.

Scarcity

  • Fixed supply cap of 21 million coins—no more will ever exist.
  • Each halving increases scarcity by permanently reducing new supply.

Past Trends

  • Historical performance after previous halvings:
    • 2012: +9,000% over next ~18 months
    • 2016: +2,800% over next ~18 months
    • 2020: +700% over next ~18 months
  • Each cycle shows similar pattern with decreasing magnitude.

Why?

  • Reduced supply meets stable/increasing demand.
  • Halvings generate media attention and investor interest.

Bottom Line

  • 2024 halving positions Sparq for strategic entry in growing crypto market.

Risk & Mitigation Strategy

Appendix
Risk Mitigation
Market Cycle Timing Risk We've built a clear market cycle map based on historical patterns, allowing us to time our TGE strategically. Our Q2 2027 target window aligns with the accumulation phase, positioned for maximum upside potential as the market enters its next bull phase. We have flexibility to adjust timing based on real-time market conditions.
Chain Selection Risk Rather than committing to a single L1/L2 blockchain prematurely, we'll run a competitive RFP process in Q4 2026 to select the optimal chain partner. This approach allows chains to compete for our large user base with grant packages (target: $2M+). We'll maintain cross-chain flexibility with multi-chain architecture to mitigate potential issues with any single blockchain.
Regulatory Risk Our staged fundraising approach includes thorough legal review at each milestone. We're building with a compliance-first mindset, with token utility designed specifically as a governance and ecosystem reward token, not a speculative asset. This approach, combined with strict KYC/AML protocols and geographic restrictions where needed, positions us to adapt to evolving regulations.
Execution Risk We're building a traditional game business with solid unit economics first, with Web3 features as an enhancement layer, not a requirement. This means our business model remains viable even without TGE execution.
